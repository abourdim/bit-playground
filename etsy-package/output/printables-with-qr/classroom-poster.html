micro:bit Playground • Classroom Guide

1
🔌

Power Up Your micro:bit

Plug in the USB cable or snap in fresh batteries. You should see a friendly X on the LED grid — that means "ready to pair!"

2
🌐

Open Playground in Chrome

Double-click index.html. Our control panel loads. No internet, no login, no waiting.

3
📡

Click Connect, Choose Your Board

A window pops up with a list. Pick BBC micro:bit [xxxxx]. The dot turns green — you are live over Bluetooth!

4
🎛️

Explore Every Tab

Paint the LEDs. Watch live temperature and sound. Spin servos. Drive the 3D buggy. Press keys 1 through 8 to jump between tools.

5
📈

Graph It, Record It, Share It

Open the Graph tab. Watch sensor lines dance. Hit Record to save a session as JSON — then show a friend what you discovered!

Every scientist started by asking "what happens if I press this?"

Your micro:bit is a tiny computer. Today, you are its boss.
